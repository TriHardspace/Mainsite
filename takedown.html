Content Takedown

DMCA and other Copyright related concerns: 
For copyright concerns, please send in detail a request to hello at tylerhoban dot com.

Your request must include the following

  • Information about the content you believe to be in violation of copyright law.
  • Information about the copyright owner.
  • Infomation about the claimant.
  • A certification you have a good faith belief that the use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A certification that the information in you notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury,  you are the owner, or an agent authorized to act on behalf of the owner, of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

Please note that some content hosted on TriHard.space is purely for archival, educational, and or historical purposes. In such cases, a counter notification will be issued.

Illegal content:

Please send your reports to hello at tylerhoban dot com, on Telegram to @TylerHoban, or via any means you see fit to contact the administration of TriHard.space.
Illegal content being hosted on TriHard.space is considered a major violation of our Terms of Service. If you encounter Illegal content within our services please report it to the best of your ability. 
Following the steps below will help in aiding the removal of such content:

  • Describe the nature of the content that constitues alleged illegalities.
  • Provide a full link to said content.
  • Provide a breif statement regarding how you found this content.
  • Include any other details you can, if you have them.

Please do NOT include a copy of said content in your request for alleged illegal content, as doing so may put you as well as the TriHard.space staff at risk.
